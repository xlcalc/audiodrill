
### Privacy Notice
last updated 04 May 2024


Audiodrill does not use cookies or collect your information. However, it utilizes third party software which may use cookies. See information on third party software in the [copyright notice](/info/?show=copyright).
Audiodrill stores some user preferences, e.g., playback rate and font size, locally in your browser. To prevent keeping your preferences in local storage, use private browsing (aka incognito mode).
