### TV channels for language learners <x-p>
<div style="line-height: 1.6em; font-size: 80%">You can listen at a slower or faster playback rate and hop a few seconds backward/forward in order to repeat or skip certain video fragments. 
Playback settings are under the player. 
Keyboard shortcuts: 
 * Press left |_Ctrl_| to slow down playback.
 * Press right |_Ctrl_| to hop backwards. 
 * For a longer skip, hold |_Alt_| while pressing Hop buttons.
Tip: You can read live captions [in Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/10538231) and in [Windows 11](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/learning-center/choose-best-caption-settings). Chrome also offers live translation.</div><hr>
